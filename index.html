About

Mitchell Berry is a techno DJ and producer known for high-impact sets built on hard bounce, acid grooves and relentless reverse basslines. Fast, dark and precise, his sound is designed for maximum movement — combining underground edge with the energy and control needed to command a full room. With releases on Elixyr, Sonic Flux and Trash Tunes, Mitchell has developed a style that is both raw and refined, balancing heavy rhythmic drive with moments of tension and release. The result is a sound that feels intense, modern and built for peak-time dancefloors. His growing profile has seen him play venues including Ministry of Sound, Engine Rooms and Platform 11, alongside performances in Croatia that marked an important step in his international progression. Whether in the club or on a larger stage, Mitchell Berry delivers sets with pace, purpose and impact.
